Meet the Founder

Lea-Janell Mitchell, MS, CSCS

Lea-Janell brings a rare and powerful combination of professional ballet experience and collegiate strength and conditioning expertise. Her unique background allows her to bridge the gap between artistry and athleticism, creating programs that are both scientifically rigorous and deeply attuned to the demands of performance.

Education & Certifications

  • Master of Science in Strength and Conditioning, with a thesis on ballet cross-training and landing mechanics.
  • BFA in Dance Performance and Ballet Pedagogy from The Hartt School.
  • Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist (CSCS) through the NSCA.
  • Additional Certifications: NASM, USAW1, FMS.

Coaching & Performance Experience

  • Coached athletes across 28 NCAA varsity teams, from DIII to D1 levels.
  • Performed in Soloist and Principal roles with Ballet Hartford and Fort Wayne Ballet.
  • International performance experience, including European tours and dancing at The White House.

Guiding Philosophy

Lea-Janell's coaching philosophy centers on building resilient, powerful performers from the ground up. She emphasizes mastering mobility, enhancing stability, and developing performance-specific power to ensure longevity and peak potential in any athletic or artistic endeavor.
